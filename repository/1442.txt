#titolo ENI aims to up output 4% yearly 2013-2016 - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - London, March 14 - ENI aims to increase oil and gas
production by 4% each year between 2013 and 2016, the Italian
fuels group said in a strategic plan presented to analysts
Thursday.     A previous plan saw annual growth of 3%. 
    The group aims to invest 56.8 billion euros, up 1.6 billion
on the previous plan.     The new projects should boost production by 700,000 barrels
daily by 2016.     It sees divestments of 10 billion euros over the four years
including natural gas infrastructure unit Snam.
