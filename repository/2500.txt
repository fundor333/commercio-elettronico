    (ANSA) - New Delhi, March 12 - Italy's ambassador to New
Delhi, Daniele Mancini, has been summoned by the Indian foreign
ministry to explain Rome's decision not to send back two Italian
marines accused of killing two Indian fishermen, diplomatic
sources told ANSA Tuesday. 
     The marines, who are charged with homicide, had been
allowed to come home to vote last month.
