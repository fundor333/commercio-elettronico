#titolo Francis 'expresses simplicity' says Vatican spokesman - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 13 - The new pope's choice of
the name Francis expresses "simplicity and evangelical witness",
Vatican Spokesman Federico Lombardi said after Argentinian
cardinal Jorge Mario Bergoglio was elected to succeed Benedict
XVI Wednesday.     Francis I, the first pope from the Americas, was elected by
cardinals after just five ballots.
