#titolo Inflation 'trolley' cheaper in February - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 12 - Prices in the so-called inflation
'trolley' of most frequently bought goods rose 2.4% annually in
February, down from a 2.7% hike in January, Istat said Tuesday.      It was the lowest rise since December 2010, the
statistics agency said.
