#titolo Paolo and Nicola Bulgari probed for fraud - ANSA English
     (see related story)
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 14 - Paolo and Nicola Bulgari are
under investigation in relation to allegations the Italian
luxury goods company hid some three billion euros in revenue
from the authorities, judicial sources said Thursday.      The brothers are the grandchildren of founder Sotirio
Bulgari and stakeholders in the company.      Another member of the Bulgari family, Francesco Trapani, is
also being probed, along with the group's legal representative
Maurizio Valentini.
