#titolo Pope Francis pledges to serve Gospel with 'renewed love' - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 15 - Pope Francis on Friday
pledged to serve the Gospel with ''renewed love'' and ''help the
Church to become ever more in Christ and with Christ'' during a
meeting with cardinals in the Vatican. 
     ''Together we, ministers and the faithful, will endeavour
to faithfully pursue the perennial mission of taking Christ to
man and of leading man to Christ, the Way, the Truth and the
Life, who is truly present in the Church and at the same time in
every human being,'' said the new Argentine pope who was elected
to succeed Benedict XVI on Wednesday.
