#titolo Italian export figures 2012 up for country's islands - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 13 - The Italian regions that
contributed most to the nation's 2012 export growth are the
country's two main islands of Sicily and Sardinia, a report by
statistics agency Istat said on Wednesday.       The northern region of Lombardy increased exports by 3.7%
compared to 2011, Tuscany 6.9%, Emilia-Romagna 3.1%.       Exports from the island of Sicily rose 21.2% and Sardinia
21.5% from the previous year.
