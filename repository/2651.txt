#titolo '10,000 Italian shops closed down in Jan-Feb' - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 15 - Some 10,000 shops closed down in
recession-hit Italy in January and February, the sharpest drop
in 20 years, small-business association Confesercenti said
Friday.     If the trend of 167 closures a day continues, it said, Italy
will have lost 60,000 shops by year's end.
