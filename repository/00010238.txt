#titolo New pope is Francis, not Francis I, says Vatican - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 14 - The Vatican on Thursday
stressed that the new pope should be called Francis, not Francis
I.      "You only need to call him Francis, not Francis I," Vatican
spokesman Father Federico Lombardi told a news conference. 
     "Once there's a Francis II you can refer to him that way".
