#titolo Vatican post office celebrates new pontificate - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 14 - The Vatican post office
launched a postmark on Thursday celebrating the election of Pope
Francis. 
     "Habemus Papam" is written on the postmark, which also
features the date March 14 and an image of the new pope greeting
the crowds from the balcony of the papal apartment.      Another Vatican postmark honors the March 12 opening of the
conclave and the image of the Sistine Chapel. 
     The Vatican post office is also offering a certificate
stamp dedicated to Saint Francis and the beginning of the new
pontificate for the restoration of the colonnade in Saint
Peter's Square.
