#titolo Italian real-estate market down to 1985 levels - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 14 - Italy's housing market dropped to
1985 levels last year, with only 444,000 units being sold
throughout the country, the Italian tax agency, Agenzia delle
Entrate, said Thursday.      Fourth-quarter sales for 2012 fell across the country 29.6%
by volume, the worst figure since 2004. 
     Mortgages were also down by 38.6%. 
     Real-estate prices are down in the country's 12 largest
cities by population, with the exception of Verona where sales
were up 0.2%.
