#titolo Pope's first Angelus Sunday, inaugural mass Tuesday - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 13 - Francis I will lead his
first Angelus as pope in St Peter's Square on Sunday, the
Vatican confirmed after cardinals elected him pope Wednesday. 
     On Thursday the Argentine pontiff will receive cardinals,
both electors who participated in the conclave and those who did
not, in the Sistine Chapel. 
     The inaugural mass of his pontificate will take place on
Tuesday.
