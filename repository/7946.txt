     (ANSA) - Rome, March 18 - United States President Barack
Obama's trip to Israel is the "only reason" he is not attending
Pope Francis's inauguration at St Peter's Tuesday,
Vice-President Joe Biden told Italian President Giorgio
Naplitano Monday.      Biden said Obama was sending his best wishes to Argentina's
Francis, the first pope from the Americas, elected Wednesday
after pope Benedict's shock abdication last month.
