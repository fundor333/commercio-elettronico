#titolo Cardinals are 'pope's priests' says Francis - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 15 - Cardinals are the pontiff's
priests, Pope Francis told them at an informal meeting isnide
the Vatican Friday.     "We are brothers, the cardinals are the priests of the Holy
Father, we live that community, that friendship and closeness
which will do us all good," the new pope told the College of
Cardinals in the Clementine Hall.     Francis, who is expected to try to end reported splits in
the Catholic Church's governing body, the Curia, stressed the
importance of Church unity.
