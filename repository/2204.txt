#titolo Puglia mafia assets seized - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Bari, March 15 - Italian police on Friday seized 100
million euros in assets from the Puglia mafia near Bari.     The assets included property, cars and bank accounts in the
name of a dead mafioso, police said.     The Sacra Corona Unita is Italy's fourth mafia after Cosa
Nostra, the Camorra and 'Ndrangheta.
