#titolo Pope Francis thanks predecessor Benedict - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Vatican City, March 15 - Pope Francis on Friday
thanked his predecessor Benedict XVI, who became the first pope
to abdicate in 600 years last month.    Francis told cardinals in the Vatican that Benedict "enriched
the Church with the magistery of faith, humility and mildness.    The new pontiff, elected Wednesday, said Benedict's legacy
"will remain a spiritual patrimony for all".     The Argentine pope said his German predecessor had shown
"total dedication to the Throne of St Peter".
