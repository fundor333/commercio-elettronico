#titolo Italians advised to be 'prudent' in India amid marines row - ANSA English
     (see related stories).    (ANSA) - Rome, March 15 - The Italian foreign ministry on
Friday advised Italians in India to show "prudence" amid
probable street protests against Italy's refusal to send back
two anti-piracy marines accused of murdering two Indian
fishermen last year.      An advisory on the 'viaggiaresicuri' (travel safe) Website
said Italians in India or planning a trip there should try to
keep away from any protests, especially in the southern state of
Kerala where the fishermen came from.
