#titolo Over 11,000 'moonlighting' public employees uncovered - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 13 - More than 11,700 state employees
and consultants received pay for unauthorized work outside of
their public contracts in 2012, Italian finance police said on
Wednesday.       A special investigative police unit monitoring compliance
with rules governing civil service and transparency in public
administration carried out probes into state-contracted workers'
for 2012.       The unit found 859 of those civil servants received
payments of approximately six million euros from a second job
that was not authorized by the government body for which they
work.
