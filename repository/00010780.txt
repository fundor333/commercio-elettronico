#titolo Maradona hopes to meet Pope Francis - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Naples, March 13 - Argentine soccer great Diego
Maradona is hoping to meet his countryman Pope Francis I, the
ex-Napoli player and former Argentina coach said after the
election of Buenos Aires Archbishop Jorge Mario Bergoglio
Wednesday.     "As soon as I come to Italy I hope to get an audience with
him," Maradona told his Italian lawyer Angelo Pisani on the
phone from Dubai.     Maradona, who won two titles with Napoli in the late 1980s
and the 1986 World Cup with Argentina, is currently a global
ambassador for the Dubai Sports Council
