#titolo Bulgari assets seized in tax probe - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Rome, March 14 - Italian police on Thursday seized
46 million euros worth of assets belonging to Italian luxury
goods company Bulgari in relation to a tax-evasion probe. 
     The assets included Bulgari's property on Rome's chic Via
dei Condotti shopping street. 
     The company is suspected of having hidden from the
authorities some three billion euros of revenue since 2006.
