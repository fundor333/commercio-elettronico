#titolo Thief seeks police help while chased by plain-clothes unit - ANSA English
     (ANSA) - Reggio Emilia, March 12 - A supermarket employee
who stole a television from the store where he worked was
arrested by police after a nighttime car chase in Reggio Emilia,
northern Italy.      Unaware the car following him belonged to a plain-clothes
police unit, the thief - afraid he was being followed by
criminals - called a police hotline to report he was being
followed.      The officer who answered, aware that his colleagues were
following the thief, told him to pull over and surrender.
